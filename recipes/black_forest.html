Quick Black Forest Cake

Description

Black Forest cake but easier

Very moist

Ingredients

  • 1 package devil's food cake mix with pudding
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon almond extract
  • 1 can cherry pie filling
  • 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Mix together: cake mix, beaten eggs, almond extract, cherry pie filling and 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips. Stir until just combined. Pour batter into a greased 9x13 inch pan.
  3. Bake in a 175 degree C oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Remove cake from oven and let cool.
  4. To Make Glaze: Heat 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips, butter or margarine, and milk in a saucepan over medium high heat. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 74 degrees C.
  5. Spread glaze over cooled cake. Serve cake as is or with whipped cream and a cherry.
